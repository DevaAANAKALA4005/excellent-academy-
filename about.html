Our Story

A School Built on Trust, Excellence & Care

Founded in 2012, Excellent Academy began with a simple vision — to create a school in Mandleshwar where every child feels valued, challenged, and inspired. Over a decade later, that vision continues to guide everything we do.

Today, we are recognized as one of the top schools in Mandleshwar, Khargone, offering a complete educational journey from kindergarten to senior secondary science, complemented by integrated competitive exam preparation.

Our success comes from balance — strong academics with joyful learning, modern facilities with personal attention, and high expectations with genuine care for every student.
